Owlyshield is opensource and distributed under the EUPL v1.2 license.
See https://joinup.ec.europa.eu/collection/eupl/eupl-text-eupl-12 for more information.

You can find the sources of the project on GitHub: https://github.com/SitinCloud/Owlyshield

The community version is fully operational and will efficiently protect your system against ransomwares.
You don't need anymore to start Windows in test-signing mode since we now provide the signed driver in the community version.

The installation and use of Owlyshield service is free of charge.

You can get a personalized assistance by accepting the sending of your data of use.

Here is the list of data that may be collected if you opt for assistance:
- hostname
- username (mail address)
- company
- country
- phone number
- event date
- event details
- nature of event
- num version
